Our Logistics Services

Flagship Service

Dry Ice Production & Supply

As the leading producer of dry ice in East Africa, we provide reliable, high-quality dry ice supply for various industrial, pharmaceutical, and commercial applications. Our state-of-the-art production facility ensures consistent quality and supply.

  • High-purity dry ice production
  • Pharmaceutical cold chain solutions
  • Food preservation and transport
  • Industrial cooling applications
  • Scheduled delivery and on-demand supply

Freight Transportation

Reliable road freight services across Uganda and East Africa. Our modern fleet of over 50 vehicles ensures safe and timely delivery of your cargo to any destination.

  • Full truckload (FTL) and less than truckload (LTL)
  • Temperature-controlled transport
  • Heavy cargo and specialized equipment
  • Real-time GPS tracking

Warehousing & Storage

Secure warehousing solutions with over 10,000 square meters of storage space. We offer flexible storage options to meet your inventory management needs.

  • Climate-controlled storage
  • Inventory management systems
  • Bonded warehousing
  • Cross-docking services

Distribution Services

Efficient last-mile delivery and distribution network covering all major cities and towns in Uganda. We ensure your products reach customers on time.

  • Same-day delivery in Kampala
  • Nationwide coverage
  • Express and standard delivery options
  • Proof of delivery documentation

Cross-Border Logistics

Seamless cross-border transportation to Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, Democratic Republic of Congo, and beyond. We handle all customs documentation.

  • Customs clearance assistance
  • Door-to-door delivery
  • Transit insurance
  • Documentation handling

Supply Chain Consulting

Expert advice to optimize your supply chain operations. We analyze and improve efficiency to reduce costs and improve delivery times.

  • Route optimization
  • Cost analysis and reduction
  • Supply chain audits
  • Strategic planning
